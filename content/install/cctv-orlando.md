### Setup & Installation
Setting up a CCTV Orlando system can be broken down into several straightforward steps:

1. **Pre-Van Consideration**: Assess where you want to place your cameras. Ideally, cover entry points and areas with high foot traffic, and ensure your Wi-Fi signal is strong if using wireless cameras.
2. **Mounting the Cameras**: CCTV Orlando provides brackets and tools for camera installation. Use a power drill to mount cameras securely. It’s advisable to place them at a height of 8-10 feet to avoid tampering.
3. **Connecting to Power**: Most systems need a power source, whether through a direct electrical line or a battery-powered option. Ensure that your power outlets are in proximity or extendable if necessary. 
4. **Network Setup**: For IP cameras, connect them to your home network via Ethernet or Wi-Fi. Ensure that all setup details are correctly entered in the app.
5. **Educate Yourself on the App**: Before finalizing your setup, download the CCTV Orlando app. Create or log in to your account and follow the app instructions to link your cameras.
6. **Testing**: After installing and connecting the cameras, run a series of tests. Check both live footage and recorded video to assess quality and functionality, adjusting the camera angles as needed.

For non-tech-savvy users, CCTV Orlando often offers professional installation services. This would remove the hassle of setup and ensure optimal camera placement.
