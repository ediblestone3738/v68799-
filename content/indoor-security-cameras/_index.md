## Choosing the Right Camera

Every home has different needs. Consider these points when choosing an indoor camera:

- **Placement:** Determine the areas you want to monitor, such as hallways, living rooms, or nurseries.  
- **Power Source:** Decide between wired, PoE, or wireless battery-powered models.  
- **Smart Features:** Look for motion alerts, cloud storage, app notifications, and smart assistant integration.  
- **Video Quality:** HD or 2K cameras provide clearer images, especially in low light.  
- **Night Vision:** Essential for 24/7 monitoring.
