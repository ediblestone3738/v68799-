### Setup & Installation
Setting up the Amcrest UltraHD Indoor Camera is surprisingly simple, making it accessible even to those who may not have extensive technical knowledge. Here’s a step-by-step guide to getting started:

1. **Unbox the Camera:** Begin by unboxing all components, ensuring you have the camera, power adapter, and necessary mounting hardware.
2. **Download the App:** Search for the Amcrest View app in your iOS or Android app store and download it. This app will serve as your control center.
3. **Power it Up:** Connect the camera to a power source using the included adapter. Wait for the camera to boot up, signaled by a flashing blue light in a steady rhythm.
4. **Connect to Wi-Fi:** Open the Amcrest View app, create an account (if needed), and follow the in-app instructions to pair your camera with Wi-Fi. Scan the QR code displayed within the app using the camera.
5. **Positioning:** Choose an optimal location for the camera that covers the desired area. Note that the camera can either be placed on a flat surface or mounted on a wall using the integrated mounting bracket.
6. **Configure Settings:** Once set up, customize your notification preferences, motion detection zones, and camera settings directly through the app to suit your monitoring requirements.
7. **Test Functionality:** Perform a quick test to ensure motion detection works and that video streaming is smooth.

With every step covered, users can have their Amcrest UltraHD Indoor Camera operational in under 30 minutes, thanks to its intuitive design.
