Setup & Installation: How to Connect Reolink Camera to WiFi

Setting up your Reolink camera is a hassle-free process. Here’s a step-by-step guide on how to connect Reolink camera to WiFi:

1. **Download the Reolink App**: The first step is to download the Reolink app, available on both Android and iOS platforms. Once downloaded, create an account or log in with your existing credentials.

2. **Connect Power Supply**: Ensure your camera is powered on by connecting it to a suitable outlet using the provided power adapter.

3. **Connect to WiFi**:
   - Start the app and select “Add Device.”
   - Once the camera is powered on, it will emit a voice prompt, indicating it is ready to connect.
   - Select the WiFi network you want your camera to connect to and enter the password. Ensure that your phone is on the same WiFi network for a seamless connection.

4. **Scan QR Code**: Follow the on-screen instructions to scan the QR code displayed on your smartphone using the camera. This serves as the bridge connecting your mobile to the camera’s WiFi.

5. **Complete Setup**: After the successful connection, customize the camera settings through the app according to your preferences. Here, you can adjust motion detection zones, alerts, and video settings.

6. **Position the Camera**: Choose a strategic location for the camera, ensuring it covers the critical areas of your environment. Mount it securely if required, paying attention to the angle and distance.

7. **Test the Feed**: After installation, check the live feed through the app. Verify that the quality, angle, and functionalities meet your expectations.

With these steps, you will have your Reolink camera connected to WiFi and fully functional in no time.
