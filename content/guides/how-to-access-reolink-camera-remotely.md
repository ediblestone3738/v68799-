Setup & Installation

Setting up your Reolink camera is a straightforward process, making it accessible even for those who aren’t tech-savvy. First, ensure your camera is connected to a power source. Most models come with a user-friendly app available on both Android and iOS, which is essential for configuring remote access.

1. **Download the Reolink App:** Start by downloading the Reolink app from your device’s app store. After installation, open the app, and create an account if you do not already have one.

2. **Add Your Camera:** Once your account is set up, connect your camera to your local Wi-Fi network through the app. This is accomplished by either scanning the camera’s QR code or entering the camera's information manually. Ensure you have your Wi-Fi credentials handy.

3. **Remote Access Configuration:** To access your Reolink camera remotely, make sure to enable remote access in your camera settings through the app. The app will guide you through any necessary steps to ensure that your camera is securely connected to Reolink’s cloud service, establishing a secure connection.

4. **Test Connectivity:** After the initial setup, test your remote access. Disconnect from your home Wi-Fi and use cellular data to log into the app. You should be able to view your camera feed without issues.

Tips for Installation:
- Position your camera within the optimal Wi-Fi range to avoid connectivity drops.
- Use the Reolink Solar Panel option for outdoor cameras to keep them powered without running external cables.
- Ensure your camera is mounted securely to avoid tampering.
