### Setup & Installation

Setting up your Reolink camera and configuring the app is a straightforward process, even for those less technically inclined. Most users can have their systems up and running within minutes by following these steps:

1. **Physical Installation**: Mount the camera at your desired location, ensuring it’s within range of your Wi-Fi network. For optimal results, install cameras in well-lit areas and out of reach from tampering.
2. **Connecting to Power**: For wired models, connect the camera to a power source using the provided adapter. For battery-operated models, ensure the device is fully charged before installation.
3. **Download the App**: Head to the App Store or Google Play Store and download the Reolink app.
4. **Create an Account**: Open the app and create a Reolink account if you don’t have one already. This allows you to manage multiple devices seamlessly.
5. **Add Your Device**: Tap the ‘Add Device’ button, then scan the QR code displayed on your camera screen or enter your device ID manually.
6. **Network Configuration**: Connect your camera to your home network by entering your Wi-Fi password. A successful connection will prompt you with a message.
7. **Settings Configuration**: Once connected, explore the settings to adjust features such as motion detection sensitivity, resolution settings, scheduled recordings, and notifications.

To ensure a smooth installation, try to minimize interference from thick walls and other electronic devices, especially for Wi-Fi connections.
