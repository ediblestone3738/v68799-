### Setup & Installation
Setting up a Reolink camera can be straightforward, provided you follow the correct steps. Here’s a simplified process to ensure successful installation:

1. **Unboxing and Initial Setup**: Begin by carefully unboxing your Reolink camera. Check all included components—camera, power adapter, cables, and mounting gear.
2. **Download the App**: For best results, download the Reolink app from the App Store or Google Play Store. This will serve as the main interface for camera management.
3. **Connect to Wi-Fi**: If you opt for a Wi-Fi model, ensure your home network is operational. Use the app to connect the camera to your Wi-Fi by having it scan the QR code displayed in the app.
4. **Physical Installation**: Choose a mounting location—ideally, high and with a clear line of sight. Use the included screws to mount the camera securely.
5. **Power On**: Connect the power adapter, and wait for the camera to boot up. You may hear a voice prompt indicating readiness.
6. **Configure Settings**: Open the app to finalize configuration settings such as motion detection zones, notification preferences, and quality settings.

Common installation errors include Wi-Fi connectivity issues or failure to recognize SD cards. Make sure your camera is in range of the Wi-Fi router and that the SD card is properly formatted before use.
