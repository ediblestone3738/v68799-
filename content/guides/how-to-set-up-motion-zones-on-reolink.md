### Setup & Installation
Setting up motion zones on Reolink cameras is straightforward. To get started, ensure your Reolink camera is power-connected, paired with the Reolink App, and positioned optimally to provide coverage of the areas you want to monitor. Here’s a step-by-step guide to help you implement the motion zones:

1. **Download the Reolink App:** Available on iOS and Android, the app is your central hub for camera management. Create an account or log in if you already have one.
2. **Add Your Camera:** Follow the prompts to add your Reolink camera to the app. Ensure that the camera is on the same Wi-Fi network as your mobile device for seamless connectivity.
3. **Access Settings:** Once your camera is set up, tap on it to access the live view. In the live view interface, locate the settings option usually represented by a gear icon on the interface.
4. **Navigate to Motion Settings:** Within the settings menu, look for ‘Motion Detection’. You’ll find options for motion settings, including the creation of motion zones.
5. **Create Motion Zones:** Here, you can create different zones. The interface allows you to drag and resize boxes overlayed on the camera's video feed. This level of customization is invaluable; it lets you adjust which areas are monitored. Highlight the zones you want the camera to notify you about while excluding areas that might generate false alerts.
6. **Test Motion Detection:** After setting up your zones, walk through them to test the functionality. Be sure to adjust the sensitivity settings as needed, which can also be done through the app.
7. **Save Your Settings:** Once everything is in place, remember to save your settings and exit the setup menu.
