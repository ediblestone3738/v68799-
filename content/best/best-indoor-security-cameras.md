## Why Choose an Indoor Security Camera?

Indoor security cameras are essential for keeping your home safe, monitoring children and pets, and enhancing peace of mind. They offer clear video, two-way audio, motion detection, and smart home integration without the need for outdoor weatherproofing. When selecting an indoor camera, consider features like resolution, field of view, motion alerts, storage options, and compatibility with smart assistants.

Choosing the right model depends on your home layout, monitoring needs, and budget. To help, we’ve tested and reviewed the most popular cameras on the market.
